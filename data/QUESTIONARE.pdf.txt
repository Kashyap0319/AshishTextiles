PACKING LIST – FOREMOST WE RECEIVE PACKING LIST IN EXCEL FORMAT FROM THE MILL . THEN WE ADD THIS DATA ON 
GOOGLE SHEETS FOR EVERYONE TO ACCESS. WE CURRENTLY USE VLOOKUP TO MATCH ARTICLE NUMBER AND ADD THE 
QUALITY LIKE LY DRILL POPLIN ON THE SHEET. ONCE THIS GETS DONE THEN WE SELECT EACH CATEGORY AND PUT RACK 
NUMBER NEXT TO IT. AFTER ALL THE RACK NUMBERS ARE SET THE SHEET IS READY.  
PHYSICAL ARRIVAL OF TRUCK – NOW WE RECEIVE THE DISPATCH PHYSICALLY. WE START UNLOADING THE GOODS AND 
THEY ARE KEPT IN AN OPEN AREA IN OUR GODOWN, ALL THE GOODS ARE MIXED RIGHT NOW AS A TRUCK HAS FOR EG 
30,000 METERS ALL DIFFERENT QUALITY ROLLS. WHEN THE TRUCK GETS UNLOADED, WE START SORTING THE QUALITIES 
OUT.  
SORTING THE GOODS – NOW IS THE TIME TO USE THE SHEETS TO SORT GOODS QUALITY WISE. A PERSON HOLDS THE 
PHYSUCAL ROL LAND READ OUT BALE NYUMBER MENTIONED ON THE STICKER/TAG. THAT BALE IS ENTERED IN THE 
GOOGLE SHEET USING FIND FUNCTION AND THEN THE PERSON ON COMPUTER SAY ALOUD THE RACK NUMBER THAT IS 
MENTION ON SHEET FOR THAT PARTICULAR ROLL. HE ALSO WRITES IT AGAIN IN A COLUMN TO DENOTE THAT THIS ROLL 
HAS BEEN CHECKED. NOT THE PERSON WITH THE ROLL WRITES THE RACK NUMBER ON IT WITH A MARKER AND LABOUR 
TAKES IT TO THAT PARTICULAR RACK.  
ONCE THE ENTIRE LIST IN COMPLETE OR THE ROLLS ARE OVER, WE CHECK IF ANY ITEM IS MISSING ON THE GOOGLE 
SHEET. IF YES WE NOTE IT DOWN TO SEE IF IT WAS SHORT FROM MILL OR IT COULD ALSO HAVE BEEN MISSED TO TICK IN 
THE SHEET. WE ADD SUCH ROLLS TO NOT FOUND IN BUSY. WE MAKE A SALE OF THESE ROLLS AND WRITE *NOT 
FOUND/TRACED* IN PARTICULARS WHILE SAVING THE SALE. THIS SALE HAS ROLLS THAT WE COULDN’T TICK IN THE 
SHEET. NOW IF IN FUTURE WHILE MAKING SALE OF A BUYER , A PARTICULAR ROLL SHOWS UP IN THIS SALE, WE KNOW 
THAT IT MIGHT HAVE BEEN MISED TO TICK IN THE SHEET SO WE DLETE IT FROM NOT FOUND SALE AND ADD IT TO THE 
BUYERS SALE WE ARE MAKING.  
SALE – WHEN A BUYER CONFIRM SOME ORDER. WE JUST MAKE SALE OF THE FABRIC ON BUSY AND DISPATCH THE 
GOODS TO THE BUYER. SALE COULD COME FROM SWATCHES, OR A BUYER VISIT AND SELECTING THE WHOLE RACK. 
YOU WILL HAVE THE SALE DATA. WE WANT YOU TO MAKE A BOT TO CHECK THE SALES TO SEE IF ANY DIFFERETN 
QUALITY ROLL IS SENT TO A BUYER. FOR EG IF THE WHOLE SALE IS OF POPLIN TO CHECK IF THERE IS ANY OTHER 
QUALITY OTHER THAN POPLIN AND FLAG IT. COZ MOSTLY WHEN WE MAKE SALES, WE TRY TO MAKE IT QUALITY WISE. 
LIKE IF A CUSTOMER PURCHASED BOTOOM TWILL, POPLIN, LYCRE POPLIN DRILL ETC. WE ALWAYS MAKE SEPARATE 
SALES FOR EACH QUALITY SO THAT WE CAN RATE ITEMS CORRECTLY AND ALSO THE CUSTOMER CAN KNOW HOW 
MUCH QTY HE HAS OF EACH CATEGORY/QUALITY. SO IF POPLIN SALE HAS A ROLL OF LYCRE TWILL QUALITY WE CAN LET 
HE BUYER OR CAN ASK THE ROLL BACK AS RATES ARE DIFFERENT.
