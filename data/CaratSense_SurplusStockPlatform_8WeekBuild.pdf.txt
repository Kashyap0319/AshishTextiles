Business Context 
You operate as a surplus textile stockist — buying leftover, end-of-lot, off-shade, and slightly 
defective fabric from manufacturers, then reselling to small businesses and traders. This is 
fundamentally different from a regular textile operation: 
• 
Stock is opportunistic: you buy what becomes available, not what you forecast 
• 
Speed matters: when surplus lands, the fastest match to the right buyer wins 
• 
Buyer knowledge is your edge: knowing which buyer wants which type of fabric, quality 
grade, and price range is the core competitive advantage 
• 
Current system: Busy (offline accounting software) with manual Excel/paper tracking for 
inventory and customer history 
This platform is designed to turn that buyer knowledge — currently in peoples’ heads — into a 
system that the entire team can use.

Module 2: Buyer Intelligence & Matching 
The core differentiator: when new surplus arrives, instantly know who to call. 
How It Works 
1. New stock lands: You enter the lot (fabric type, quality, meters, price range). 
2. System matches: Based on past purchase history, the system surfaces the top 5–10 
buyers most likely to want this exact type of fabric. 
3. Team acts: Salesperson sees the match list, sends samples to the right buyers first. No 
more guessing. 
4. Feedback loop: When the buyer purchases (or passes), the system learns and 
improves future matches. 
Matching Logic 
• 
Product affinity: buyers who have previously bought this fabric type or similar fabrics 
rank highest 
• 
Quality tolerance: some buyers only want first-quality, others actively seek 
seconds/off-shade — the system knows 
• 
Volume match: do not send 10,000m lots to a buyer who typically buys 500m 
• 
Recency: buyers who purchased recently are more active than those dormant for 
months 
• 
Specialist vs generalist: specialists get matched on exact fabric type; generalists get 
matched on broader fabric category

Our Recommendations (Value-Adds Beyond the Ask) 
Based on what we have seen in your data and business model, here are features we think will 
add real value: 
★ Duplicate buyer detection 
Your data has buyers listed as “B A G” and “BAG”, “Blue falls” and “Blue fall”, “JANI TEXTILES 
LLP” and “JANI TEX LLP”. The system will auto-detect and merge duplicates so buyer history is 
complete, not split across entries. 
★ Stock aging & markdown engine 
Surplus that sits loses value. The system tracks days-since-purchase for every lot and suggests 
progressive markdowns: 15% off at 30 days, 25% at 60, clearance at 90. Thresholds are 
configurable. This turns slow inventory into cash faster. 
★ WhatsApp broadcast integration 
When new stock arrives and buyers are matched, auto-generate a WhatsApp message with stock 
details (fabric, meters, quality, price) ready to send to the matched buyer list. One tap, not 20 
minutes of manual messaging. 
★ Busy accounting data sync 
Automated import of sales and purchase data from Busy software. Since Busy is offline, we will 
set up a scheduled export-and-import flow so your platform stays current without manual re-entry. 
★ Supplier / source tracking 
Track which manufacturers you source from, what quality you typically get from each, and build a 
supplier scorecard. Over time this helps you prioritize which manufacturer’s surplus to chase. 
★ Buyer dormancy alerts 
Flag buyers who used to order regularly but have gone quiet for 30+ days. These are 
re-engagement opportunities — send them a sample of something new.
